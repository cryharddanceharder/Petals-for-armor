Petals for Armor is the critically acclaimed solo album from singer-songwriter and Paramore frontwoman Hayley Williams. An ethereal and emotionally vulnerable solo debut, Petals has a metacritic score of 83 and is lauded as one of the 'greatest albums in recorded history' according to highly important music critic, Rebecca Foster. With her powerful vocals and exquisitive lyricism, Hayley Williams remains one of Rock's most iconic women and the definition of 'absolute queen'.
